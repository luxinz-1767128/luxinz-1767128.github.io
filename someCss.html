Intro of UW

According to the UW website, "The UW is one of the world's preeminent public universities. Our impact on individuals, our region and the world is profound - whether we are launching young people into a boundless future or confronting the grand challenges of our time through undaunted research and scholarship. Ranked No. 14 in the world on the 2018 Academic Ranking of World Universities, the UW educates more than 54,000 students annually. We turn ideas into impact and transform lives and our world. For more about our impact, visit our news site, UW News."

The UW is a multi-campus university in Seattle, Tacoma and Bothell, as well as a world-class academic medical center. The UW's colleges and schools offer 1,800 undergraduate courses each quarter. We confer more than 12,000 bachelor's, master's, doctoral and professional degrees annually.
